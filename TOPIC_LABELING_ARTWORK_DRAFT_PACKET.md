## 2. Topic Selection Rationale

Labeling & Artwork is the next recommended topic because it connects directly to Packaging and Serialization. It affects barcode placement, artwork space, human-readable information, product presentation, packaging execution, and downstream readability across distribution, returns, recalls, and market-specific requirements. It is also highly cross-functional, involving Supply Chain, Regulatory, Quality, Commercial, CDMO / CMO, artwork vendors, and packaging suppliers.
